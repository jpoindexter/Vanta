Human Expertise in the Age of AI

A workshop on creativity, judgment, and what happens to our thinking when answers become instant.

FormatParticipatory
AudienceDesigners & researchers
PreparationNone required

Premise

AI can help us write, brainstorm, synthesize, and decide faster. But speed changes the shape of thought. This workshop creates room to notice what we gain, what we lose, and what we choose to protect.

This is not

Anti-AI polemic✕
Productivity seminar✕
Prompt engineering session✕

This is

A space for thoughtful practitioners to pause, reflect, and explore human expertise in a world optimized for speed.

Inquiry map

Questions worth slowing down for.

01

Where does AI genuinely help us think better?

02

03

What kinds of creative friction are worth preserving?

04

How does collaboration change when AI enters the room?

05

What parts of thinking are worth protecting?

Why now

Designers, researchers, and creative practitioners are among the first to work alongside AI every day.

Why this room

Few of us get to step back and ask what that relationship is doing to how we think together.
