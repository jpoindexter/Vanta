ND / executive-function vision — the filter

Argo's sharpened product vision: a trusted personal AI operator FOR neurodivergent people — built to support executive function and other ND traits (broad ND: dyslexia, dyscalculia, autism, ADHD, aphantasia, and more — not just autism+ADHD). This sharpens the manifesto's 'trusted digital operator' with a who and a why. Grounded in Argo's existing neurodivergent-first brain identity and the operator's own profile. The kernel-safety thesis (safe by construction) is itself an ND feature: it removes the vigilance burden.

Fit filters

F1 Initiation friction — Reduces task activation cost — lowers the 'getting started' barrier (the core ADHD/exec-function wall).
F2 Externalized memory — Offloads working memory — remembers goals, context, where you left off, what was decided.
F3 Calm output — Low-arousal, low-firehose, sensory-regulating output — never a wall of noise.
F4 Low decision load — Sensible defaults; doesn't demand constant micro-choices (decision fatigue / analysis paralysis).
F5 Structure & follow-through — Breaks down, sequences, nudges, tracks — supports planning and follow-through deficits.
F6 Reliability / trust — Predictable, safe, honest — removes the vigilance burden. Safe-by-construction = ND feature.

Curation bias

RUTHLESS by design. Six escalating 'add to goal' messages is itself scope creep — so this audit's job is a SMALL curated pull-set and a LOT of explicit DROP. A feature being slick in a dev-tool (Goose) or broad in Hermes does not make it fit an ND executive-function assistant. Default verdict is DROP unless it scores ≥2 ND filters with low cognitive cost.
