# Vanta Website Design System

## Direction

Vanta is a trusted local operator, not a generic AI dashboard. The public site uses a single black field, bone-white ink, engraved raven imagery, and one violet signal. Space, typography, product evidence, and the supplied brand artwork create the rhythm; alternating section colors, gradients, glow, decorative cards, and status colors do not.

## Palette

- Vanta Black: `#050507`
- Bone White / Ink: `#F4EFE3`
- Signal Violet: `#7C3AED`
- Green, amber, and red are reserved for real application status states.

## Typography

- Display: Sentient 400/500
- Interface and body: Switzer 400/500/600
- Technical labels: SF Mono or platform monospace
- Display text is reserved for decisive statements. Interface labels stay compact and direct.

## Layout

- Maximum content width: 78rem
- Desktop gutter: 2rem
- Mobile gutter: 1rem
- Major sections use 8rem vertical separation on desktop and 4rem on mobile.
- Product media is shown as evidence without fake browser chrome.
- Functional lists use tabular rows and structural separators, not card grids.

## Interaction

- Violet marks active tabs, focus, and the tiny brand signal only.
- Hover changes tone; elements do not lift or scale.
- Motion is limited to a short product-screen crossfade and is removed when reduced motion is requested.
- All controls retain visible keyboard focus and stable dimensions.

## Brand Mark

The full raven poster is the signature visual. The compact bracketed V is used for navigation, favicon, and small-format applications where the raven cannot remain legible.
